Why should I upgrade?
The most significant reason to upgrade your account is to increase the number of discs that you can place on the marketplace simultaneously. Discs that are marked for the marketplace (For Sale / For Trade) will receive the most visibility on disc|zump, making it easier for your discs to be found. By upgrading your account, you will be able to increase your market cap and bring more publicity to your marketable discs.
What if I change my paid membership?
You can change your account type at any time. If you are upgrading, additional charges will be prorated and your account change will take effect immediately. If you are downgrading, your account change will take place at the end of your current billing cycle.
