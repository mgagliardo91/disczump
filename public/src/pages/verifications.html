Verification Process
In order to claim your official PDGA Number, disc|zump requires a one-time verification to ensure that you own the account. This process will require you to log in using your PDGA.com account username and password. A successful login will provide us with your assigned PDGA Number and the verification will be complete. Your PDGA.com account credentials will not be stored and the only information we will obtain is the PDGA Number on your account.
Why Verify?
While PDGA verification is completely optional, it will provide other members and disc|zump visitors with the knowledge that you are who you say you are. Trust is key in successful transactions.

Verification Process
In order to verify your Facebook account, disc|zump requires that you have linked your account. The linking process will require you to log into Facebook and give disc|zump permission to access basic information on your account (email, name, profile picture). disc|zump will NEVER post on your behalf without permission and you will be able to unlink your account at any time.

Once your Facebook account is linked, verification is as simple as enabling disc|zump to make your Facebook URL public.
Why Verify?
On any site that allows users to provide a service (i.e. sell and trade discs), trust is a key component. While providing potential customers with your Facebook profile does not explicitly prove you are trustful, it does provide a sense of reassurance that you are a physical, relatable person.
