Field Description Example
Brand Enter the brand of the disc. (This field is required) Innova
Name Enter the name/mold of the disc. (This field is required) Destroyer
Type Select the type of the disc. Distance Driver
Material Enter the material of the disc. Most brands have at least 3 distinct material types. Champion
Weight Enter the weight of the disc in grams. The weight is frequently printed or written on the underside of the flight plate.
NOTE: This field only accepts positive integers with up to 3 digits, and weights typically range from 150 to 180.		 172
Color Enter the color of the disc. Orange
