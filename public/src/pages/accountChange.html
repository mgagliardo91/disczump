WE HAVE DISABLED TIERED ACCOUNTS
As of February 2018, disc|zump no longer has tiered accounts and, instead, full functionality is available to all users for free.
Why did we do this?
Because, why not? Have fun and enjoy the site.
How does this affect me?
There is no limitation on how many discs can be placed on the marketplace. Simply mark your disc as "For Sale" or "For Trade" and it will show up on the explore page for all to see.
